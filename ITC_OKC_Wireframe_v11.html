BLOCK 7c, CAPABILITY DETAIL 🆕 CAROLE'S CONTENT
How each capability works
Knowledge Continuity
Identifies where critical knowledge and judgment sit in a few key people and puts practical mechanisms in place to retain it before roles change or people leave.
How it works
  • Diagnose roles and processes at highest risk if someone exited tomorrow
  • Facilitate experts to surface real know-how, shortcuts, judgment calls, relationships
  • Convert that into usable assets: playbooks, decision guides, mentoring and shadowing routines
Outcomes
  • Reduced risk when experienced people retire or move
  • Faster onboarding and smoother role transitions
  • Critical work no longer dependent on one person
Digital Readiness
Targets the human side of digital initiatives so people are truly ready to work differently, not just trained on new tools.
How it works
  • Map how roles, workflows, and decisions change with new technology
  • Define "ready" for each role and level, beyond training completion
  • Build a readiness plan that aligns communication, learning, and leader behaviors
Outcomes
  • Higher adoption of new tools and platforms
  • Less disruption when new technology goes live
  • Technology investments tied to real behavior change
Operational Effectiveness & Process Improvement
Reveals how work actually gets done, then simplifies processes and handoffs so people can perform without constant firefighting.
How it works
  • Observe and map the real workflow with the people who do the work
  • Surface friction points, rework, and unclear handoffs
  • Guide teams to select and implement a small set of high-impact changes
Outcomes
  • Fewer delays, handoff issues, and workarounds
  • Better use of capacity and talent
  • Visible performance gains that build momentum
Frontline Capability & Performance Architecture
Gives supervisors a clear picture of what good looks like and practical tools to manage performance and coach their teams.
How it works
  • Clarify supervisor responsibilities and what good looks like in this specific context
  • Design simple tools and routines: expectations, huddles, 1:1s, basic metrics
  • Build capability so supervisors use these tools confidently in real situations
Outcomes
  • Stronger, more confident frontline leaders
  • Clearer accountability and fewer mixed messages
  • Performance managed through daily routines, not just annual reviews
Knowledge Architecture & Accountability Design
Makes the invisible org chart visible by clarifying who owns what and how work and decisions flow across teams.
How it works
  • Map critical workflows against roles and decision rights
  • Identify overlaps, gaps, and bottlenecks in accountability
  • Support leaders in making and communicating clear ownership choices
Outcomes
  • Clearer accountability across functions
  • Smoother execution during restructures or leadership changes
  • Less time spent escalating and chasing decisions
AI Workflow Readiness & Knowledge Architecture
Prepares people, processes, and knowledge so AI tools can safely and meaningfully augment human judgment.
How it works
  • Identify realistic AI use cases in current workflows
  • Define the knowledge, rules, and boundaries AI depends on
  • Document and structure institutional know-how and design AI-enabled routines
Outcomes
  • AI that is actually used and useful
  • Reduced risk from unmanaged or ad-hoc AI use
  • Human expertise amplified rather than replaced
